These questions help identify what's missing and what would fulfill you outside of work.

# Answer Value Mapping

Each answer maps to certain life path categories:

**Missing in Life:**
- Creativity → Creative Arts, Building & Making
- Physical → Physical & Sports, Adventure
- Social → Social & Community, Performance
- Peace → Spiritual & Mindfulness

**Free Time:**
- Determines time commitment compatibility
- 2-5 hours → Low time commitment paths
- 20+ hours → Can handle intensive hobbies

**Energy Level:**
- Drained → Calming, low-intensity paths
- Energized → Intense physical/social paths

**Social Needs:**
- Solo → Writing, meditation, solo sports
- Large social → Team sports, theater, meetups

And so on for all 25 questions...
